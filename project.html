+

Yes! Baoiams certificates are blockchain-verified and recognized by 100+ companies including Google, Microsoft, Infosys, TCS, Wipro, and more. They include a unique verification link that employers can verify instantly.

No prior experience is required for most beginner-level courses. Each course clearly lists prerequisites. We have paths designed for complete beginners, intermediate learners, and experienced professionals looking to upskill.

Once enrolled in a course or learning path, you get access to the internship portal. You can apply directly to partner companies, track your application status, and even get interview prep support from our career team.
